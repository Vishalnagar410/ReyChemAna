# LIMS User Guide for Chemists

Simple guide for submitting and tracking analysis requests.

## Logging In

1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Edge, or Firefox)
2. Go to: `http://[SERVER_IP]:8000` (ask IT for SERVER_IP)
   - Or double-click the **LIMS Launcher** icon on your desktop
3. Enter your **username** and **password**
4. Click **Sign In**

> You will automatically be logged out after 8 hours. Just log in again.

---

## Submitting a New Analysis Request

### Step 1: Create Request

1. After logging in, you'll see your dashboard
2. Click the **+ New Request** button (top right)

### Step 2: Fill in Request Details

1. **Compound Name**: Enter the compound ID or name
   - Example: `ABC-12345` or `Compound A`

2. **Analysis Types**: Check all analyses you need
   - Can select multiple (HPLC, NMR, LCMS, etc.)
   - Check at least ONE

3. **Priority**: Select urgency
   - **Low**: Routine, no rush
   - **Medium**: Normal workflow (default)
   - **High**: Need results soon
   - **Urgent**: Drop everything!

4. **Due Date**: When do you need results?
   - Click the calendar icon
   - Must be today or future date

5. **Description** (optional): Extra details
   - Special handling instructions
   - Purity targets
   - Specific concerns

6. **Comments** (optional): Notes for analyst
   - Sample location
   - Previous run info

### Step 3: Submit

1. Review your information
2. Click **Create Request**
3. You'll see a confirmation with your **Request Number** (e.g., REQ-0042)

> **Write down your request number!** You'll use this to track status.

## Editing Your Own Requests

### Can I edit myrequest?

**YES** - Before analyst starts work (Pending status)  
**NO** - Once analyst picks it up (In Progress or Completed)

### How to Edit

1. Go to your dashboard
2. Click on the **Pending** request
3. Make your changes
4. Click **Save Changes**

> If you need to change a request that's already in progress, contact the analyst or admin.

---

## Common Questions

**Q: How long until my request is picked up?**  
A: Depends on analyst workload. Urgent requests get priority. Check your dashboard for status updates.

**Q: I made a mistake in my request. What do I do?**  
A: If it's still Pending, edit it yourself. If In Progress, ask the analyst or admin.

**Q: Can I submit multiple analysis types for the same compound?**  
A: Yes! Check all the analyses you need when creating the request.

**Q: I need to cancel a request. How?**  
A: Contact admin or the assigned analyst.

**Q: Where are my old requests?**  
A: All your past requests are on your dashboard. Scroll down to see older ones.

**Q: Can I see other chemists' requests?**  
A: No, you can only see your own requests.

**Q: My session expired. What happened?**  
A: You were logged out after 8 hours of inactivity. Just log in again.

**Q: I forgot my password.**  
A: Contact your administrator to reset it.
